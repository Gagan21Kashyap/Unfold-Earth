    Belize is a small Caribbean country in Central America known for its coral reefs, tropical forests, and Mayan history.

      Famous Cities

    • Belize City – Largest city and cultural center
    • San Ignacio – Gateway to Mayan ruins
    • Dangriga – Known for Garifuna culture

      Popular Attractions

    • Great Blue Hole – Famous diving site
    • Belize Barrier Reef – Second-largest coral reef

      Belizean Food

    • Rice and beans with seafood
    • Caribbean and Creole cuisine

      Natural beauty

    • Caribbean beaches and islands
    • Tropical rainforests and wildlife

      Why Visit Belize?

    • Peaceful and clean country
    • Beautiful mosques
    • Unique culture
