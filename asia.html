  • India
    •

    India is a vast and diverse country in South Asia, known for its rich history,
    cultures, traditions, and landscapes ranging from mountains to beaches.

      Famous Cities

    • New Delhi – Capital city with historical monuments
    • Mumbai – Financial capital and city of dreams
    • Jaipur – Pink City known for palaces and forts
    • Varanasi – One of the oldest spiritual cities

      Popular Attractions

    • Taj Mahal – One of the Seven Wonders
    • Qutub Minar – Historic monument
    • The Himalayas in the north
    • Beaches in Goa and Kerala
    • Forests, deserts, and rivers across the country

      Indian Food

    • Diverse cuisines with spices
    • Popular dishes: curry, biryani, dosa
    • Street food culture is very famous

      Culture And Lifestyle

    • Many religions, languages, and traditions
    • Festivals like Diwali and Holi
    • Rich heritage and traditions

      Why Visit India?

    • Cultural diversity
    • Historical monuments
    • Variety of landscapes
  • Bhutan

    Bhutan is a peaceful country in the Himalayas, known for its
    natural beauty, monasteries, and happiness-focused lifestyle.

      Famous Cities

    • Thimphu – Capital city
    • Paro – Known for monasteries and airport
    • Punakha – Famous for its dzong

      Popular Attractions

    • Tiger's Nest Monastery– Cliffside monastery
    • Punakha Dzong – Beautiful fortress
    • Mountain landscapes of the Himalayas
    • Rivers, forests, and valleys

      Bhutanese Food

    • Ema Datshi (chili and cheese dish)
    • Rice-based meals
    • Simple and traditional food

      Culture and Lifestyle

    • Strong Buddhist traditions
    • Focus on Gross National Happiness
    • Peaceful and eco-friendly lifestyle

      Why Visit Bhutan?

    • Peaceful environment
    • Stunning mountains
    • Unique culture
