    • Freeport – Popular beach destination
    • Marsh Harbour – Gateway to the Abaco Islands

    • Atlantis Paradise Island – Famous luxury resort
    • Exuma Islands – Known for swimming pigs and clear waters

    • Seafood dishes

      Natural beauty

    • Turquoise ocean waters
    • Coral reefs and tropical islands

    • Seafood dishes
    • Creole cuisine

      Natural Beauty

    • Volcanic mountains and rainforests
    • Tropical beaches and waterfalls

      Why Visit Saint Lucia ?

    • Dramatic mountain scenery
    • Romantic tropical beaches
    • Luxury resorts and nature
