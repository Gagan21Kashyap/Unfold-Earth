About Christ the Redeemer

Christ the Redeemer is one of the most famous landmarks in Brazil. The statue stands on the summit of Mount Corcovado in Rio de Janeiro and overlooks the entire city.

Completed in 1931, the statue is 30 meters tall and represents Jesus Christ with open arms, symbolizing peace and welcome. It is one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
