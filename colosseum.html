About the Colosseum

The Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater located in Rome, Italy. Built in 80 AD, it was used for gladiator battles, public spectacles, and dramatic performances.

It remains one of the most iconic symbols of the Roman Empire and attracts millions of visitors each year.
