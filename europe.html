Europe

  • France
    •

    France is one of the most visited countries in the world,
    known for its historic landmarks, art, culture, and cuisine.

      Famous Cities

    • Paris – Capital city famous for the Eiffel Tower and museums
    • Nice – Coastal city on the French Riviera
    • Lyon – Known for food and historic architecture

      Popular Attractions

    • Eiffel Tower – Iconic landmark in Paris
    • Louvre Museum – One of the largest museums in the world
    • Mont Saint-Michel – Famous island abbey

      French Food

    • Croissants and baguettes
    • Cheese and wine

      Natural beauty

    • Beautiful countryside and vineyards
    • Mediterranean beaches and mountains

      Why Visit France?

    • Famous landmarks and museums
    • Romantic cities
    • World-class cuisine
  • Italy
    •

    Italy is famous for its ancient history, art, architecture, and delicious food.

      Famous Cities

    • Rome – Historic capital with ancient ruins
    • Venice – City of canals and gondolas
    • Florence – Center of Renaissance art

      Popular Attractions

    • Colosseum – Ancient Roman amphitheater
    • Leaning Tower of Pisa – Famous tilted tower
    • Amalfi Coast – Beautiful coastal scenery

      Costa Rican Food

    • Pizza and pasta
    • Gelato and Italian coffee

      Natural Beauty

    • Scenic countryside and hills
    • Mediterranean coastlines

      Why Visit Italy?

    • Ancient Roman history
    • Famous art and architecture
    • Delicious cuisine

  • Switzerland
    •

    Switzerland is famous for its mountains, lakes, and beautiful alpine scenery.

      Famous Cities

    • Zurich – Largest city and financial center
    • Geneva – International city near Lake Geneva
    • Lucerne – Scenic city with historic bridges

      Popular Attractions

    • Matterhorn Mountain – One of the most famous peaks in the Alps
    • Jungfraujoch – “Top of Europe” mountain railway

      Swiss Food

    • Cheese fondue
    • Swiss chocolate

      Natural Beauty

    • Snow-covered Alps mountains
    • Crystal-clear lakes and valleys

      Why Visit Switzerland?

    • Stunning alpine scenery
    • Outdoor adventure activities
    • Beautiful cities and villages
