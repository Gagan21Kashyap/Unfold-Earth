About the Great Wall

The Great Wall of China is one of the most impressive architectural achievements in human history. Stretching over 21,000 kilometers, it was built over several centuries to protect Chinese kingdoms from invasions.

Today, it is one of the most visited landmarks in the world and offers breathtaking mountain views along its ancient pathways.
