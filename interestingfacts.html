🌍 Interesting Facts Around The World

✨ Amazing Country Facts

India 🇮🇳

India has the world's largest democracy and the Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

France 🇫🇷

France is the most visited country in the world and is famous for the Eiffel Tower.

Japan 🇯🇵

Japan has more than 6,800 islands and is known for its advanced technology.

Brazil 🇧🇷

Brazil is home to the Amazon rainforest, the largest rainforest in the world.

Canada 🇨🇦

Canada has more lakes than the rest of the world combined.

Australia 🇦🇺

Australia is wider than the moon and has naturally pink lakes.

Italy 🇮🇹

Italy has the highest number of UNESCO World Heritage Sites.

Egypt 🇪🇬

The Great Pyramid of Giza is the oldest of the Seven Wonders.

USA 🇺🇸

The USA has no official language at the federal level.

China 🇨🇳

The Great Wall of China is over 21,000 km long.

South Africa 🇿🇦

It has three capital cities: Pretoria, Cape Town, and Bloemfontein.

Maldives 🇲🇻

The Maldives is the lowest country in the world.
