💡 EXPERT ADVICE

Smart Tips for Perfect Itineraries

01

Book Flights Early

Reserve international flights 4-6 months ahead to save up to 40% on tickets.

02

Balance Activities

Mix high-energy days with relaxed downtime so you never feel rushed.

03

Use Local Apps

Download offline maps and translation tools before you land.

04

Build in Buffer Time

Always leave 1-2 flexible days for spontaneous discoveries.

05

Pack Light & Smart

Follow the 3-2-1 rule: 3 tops, 2 bottoms, 1 pair of versatile shoes.

06

Track Your Budget

Use a shared Google Sheet to split expenses easily with travel companions.

07

Stay Connected Safely

Get a local eSIM and enable two-factor authentication everywhere.

08

Document Everything

Take daily notes or photos — you'll thank yourself when reliving the trip.
