About Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza is one of the most famous Mayan archaeological sites located in Mexico's Yucatán Peninsula. It flourished between the 9th and 12th centuries.

The central structure, El Castillo Pyramid, is famous for its astronomical design and symmetry.
