About Petra

Petra is an ancient archaeological city located in Jordan. It is famous for its stunning rock-cut architecture and water conduit system built by the Nabataeans over 2000 years ago.

The most iconic structure in Petra is the Treasury, a magnificent temple carved directly into pink sandstone cliffs.
